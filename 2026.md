The Reproducible AGILE team is happy to contribute to the AGILE conference 2026 by organising the reproducibility review, continueing an exemplary practice in Open Scholarship from the previous years.
For the seventh time, a reproducibility review is conducted with all accepted full papers based on the mandatory [AGILE Reproducible Paper Guidelines](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/CB7Z8).
As started in 2025, non-compliance with the guidelines, i.e., not including a Data and Software Availability section in the submission, will lead to a desk reject.
